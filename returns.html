Returns & Refunds Policy

Our commitment to your satisfaction with BeautyTech products

Our Guarantee

At BeautyTech, we stand behind the quality of our products. We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all purchases because we want you to have complete confidence in your skincare tools. If you're not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're not completely satisfied with your UltraGlow Pro or any BeautyTech product, you can return it within 30 days of receipt for a full refund of the purchase price or an exchange. This guarantee applies to products purchased directly from BeautyTech.com or authorized retailers.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

Condition Requirement
Time Frame Within 30 days of receipt
Product Condition Must be in original condition with minimal use
Packaging Original packaging preferred but not required
Documentation Proof of purchase (order number, receipt)

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Replacement brush heads or accessories that have been opened or used
  • Gift cards
  • Products marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Items showing signs of excessive use, damage, or tampering

Return Process

We've made our return process as simple as possible. Follow these steps to return your BeautyTech product:

1

Initiate Your Return

Contact our customer service team at support@beautytech.com or call (555) 123-4567 to receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number. Please have your order number ready.

2

Package Your Return

Place the item(s) in secure packaging. Include your RMA number and a copy of your receipt or order confirmation. If possible, use the original packaging.

3

Ship Your Return

Send your return to the address provided by our customer service team. We recommend using a trackable shipping method. Keep your tracking information until your return is processed.

4

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return (usually within 3-5 business days), we'll process your refund. You'll receive an email notification when your refund has been processed.

Refund Timeframe

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. Please note that it may take an additional 2-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment method and financial institution.

Refunds & Exchanges

Refund Options:

  • Original Payment Method: Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.
  • Store Credit: If you prefer, we can issue store credit for the full purchase amount plus an additional 10% bonus.
  • Gift Returns: Returns of items received as gifts will be issued as store credit to the recipient.

Exchange Policy:

If you'd like to exchange your product for a different model or color, please follow the same return process and indicate your exchange preference when contacting customer service. We'll help you process the exchange as quickly as possible.

Return Shipping Costs

For returns due to customer preference (wrong color, changed mind, etc.), the customer is responsible for return shipping costs. For defective products or our error in shipping, BeautyTech will cover return shipping costs and send a prepaid return label.
