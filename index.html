How to Use Your UltraGlow Pro

Follow these simple steps to get the most out of your UltraGlow Pro Facial Cleansing Brush:

Prepare Your Skin

Dampen your face with warm water and apply your favorite cleanser directly to your skin or onto the brush head.

Turn On and Select Mode

Press the power button once to turn on. Press the mode button to cycle through the 5 intensity levels. We recommend starting with level 1 or 2 for sensitive skin.

Cleanse in Zones

Gently move the brush in circular motions across your face. The built-in timer will pulse every 20 seconds to remind you to move to a different area. Focus on T-zone, cheeks, and chin.

Rinse and Pat Dry

Rinse your face with water and pat dry with a clean towel. The UltraGlow Pro will automatically turn off after 2 minutes.

Clean Your Brush

Rinse the brush head thoroughly with warm water after each use. Allow to air dry completely before storing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use the UltraGlow Pro?

For most skin types, we recommend using the UltraGlow Pro once daily, preferably in the evening to remove makeup and impurities accumulated throughout the day. If you have sensitive skin, you may want to start with 2-3 times per week and gradually increase frequency as your skin adjusts.

Can I use the UltraGlow Pro with any cleanser?

The UltraGlow Pro works with most gel, foam, and cream cleansers. We recommend avoiding cleansers with large exfoliating particles as these can be too abrasive when used with the brush. Oil-based cleansers are also compatible, but may reduce the lifespan of the silicone bristles over time.

How do I know when to replace the brush head?

The silicone brush head is designed to last much longer than traditional nylon bristle brushes. We recommend replacing it every 6-12 months, depending on usage frequency. Signs that you need a replacement include discoloration that doesn't wash away, permanent deformation of the bristles, or reduced effectiveness.
