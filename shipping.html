Important Notice for International Customers

Please be aware that international orders may be subject to additional import duties, taxes, and customs clearance fees imposed by your country's customs authorities. These fees are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our shipping charges. Delivery times for international orders are estimates and may vary due to customs processing.

Order Processing

We strive to process and ship orders as quickly as possible:

  • Orders placed before 1 PM EST on business days are typically processed the same day.
  • Orders placed after 1 PM EST or on weekends/holidays will be processed the next business day.
  • During peak seasons (holidays) or promotional periods, processing times may be extended by 1-2 business days.

You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been shipped. Please allow up to 24 hours after receiving your order confirmation for your tracking information to become active.

Shipping Restrictions

We currently do not ship to:

  • P.O. Boxes
  • APO/FPO addresses
  • Certain countries due to international regulations (please contact customer support for specific country information)

Shipping FAQ

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with your tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your BeautyTech account and viewing your order history.

What should I do if my package is delayed?

If your tracking information hasn't updated in 48 hours or your package appears to be delayed beyond the estimated delivery date, please contact our customer support team at support@beautytech.com or call (555) 123-4567. We'll investigate the status of your shipment and provide assistance.

Do you offer Saturday delivery?

Saturday delivery is available for Next-Day shipping in select metropolitan areas. This option will be displayed at checkout if it's available for your shipping address. An additional fee of $5.99 applies for Saturday delivery.

What happens if my package is lost or damaged?

In the rare event that your package is lost or arrives damaged, please contact our customer support team within 5 business days of the expected delivery date. We'll work with the shipping carrier to file a claim and arrange for a replacement to be sent as soon as possible.
